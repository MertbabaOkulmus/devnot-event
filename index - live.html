Tech Leaders Summit is one of the most important technology leaders summits in Turkey. This event, featuring high-level speakers and panelists, focuses on leadership, management, transformation and new technological developments.

The Tech Leaders Summit will take place from April 15, 2026 at Radisson Blu Şişli Hotel in İstanbul. You can find detailed information in the Location & Venue section just above.
